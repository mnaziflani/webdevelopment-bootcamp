Top 3 Movies

Terminator

Terminator is a science fiction film released in 1984, directed by James Cameron. It stars Arnold Schwarzenegger as a cyborg assassin sent back in time to kill Sarah Connor, the mother of the future leader of the human resistance against machines.

Wall-E

Wall-E is a 2008 animated science fiction film produced by Pixar Animation

The Conjuring

The Conjuring is a 2013 supernatural horror film directed by James Wan. It is based on the real-life paranormal investigations of Ed and Lorraine Warren, focusing on their work with the Perron family, who experienced terrifying events in their Rhode Island farmhouse during the 1970s.
